Drink Around the World!

Are you ready for a drunken adventure around the world?

Welcome to Mexico!


The Mexican pavillion is home to a gorgeous indoor market inside a Mayan temple building. Don't miss the Three Caballeros boat ride!


Drink Recommendation:


Stop by La Cava del Tequila for a tequila flight to start the day with a bang! There is also a variety of margaritas if you'd like a less intense start to the day

Ready to move on?


Welcome to Norway!


The Norway pavillion is home to the new Frozen Ever After ride that you won't want to miss. A fast pass is highly recommended for this ride!


Drink Recommendation:


A Viking Coffee (Bailey's Irish Cream and Kamora Coffee Liqueur) from Kringla Bakeri Og Cafe is a great way to keep your energy up!

Ready to move on?


Welcome to China!


These is lots of delicious food in the China Pavillion. Reflections of China is a must-see film presentation shown in a standing circle vision theater.


Drink Recommendation:


A Green Tea Plum Slush from the Tea Stand is a great way to beat the heat!

Ready to move on?


Welcome to Germany!


Beer is the obvious choice in the Germany pavillion, but don't miss out on a giant pretzel or some 'wurst! Make sure to head deeper into the pavillion to avoid long lines.


Drink Recommendation:


Altenmunster Oktoberfest or a Jagermeister shot if you're feeling feisty!

Welcome to Italy!


The Italy pavillion features live street theatre as well as singers to entertain guests.


Drink Recommendation:


Frizzante (Campari, mandarin orange juice and Prosecco)
